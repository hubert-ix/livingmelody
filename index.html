Hubert Razack
Tenor, composer, producer

With over 20 years of experience in the music industry, Hubert has established himself as a versatile tenor, composer, and music producer, crafting and performing beautiful melodies across various genres, from electronic to classical.

Born and raised in France, he now resides in Toronto, Canada, where he continues to produce and create music in a variety of genres.

"Fauré", a collection of 4 songs performed by Tenor Hubert Razack and pianist Cari Astleford, is now available on all streaming platforms.

Check out the video for "Au bord de l'eau", featuring Hubert, his friend Wilbur, and a whole load of mustaches.

Inspirium is out!

Introducing "Inspirium", a beautiful instrumental piece of music that takes listeners on a journey through the sounds of acoustic guitars, bass, harp, percussions, and a variety of hand played instruments. Featuring talented guest musicians on flute, violin, and trumpet, Inspirium is a captivating journey through time and sound.

It's available on all streaming platforms and you can also purchase it on BandCamp.

Collaboration for Ukraine

Hubert has participated in a re-recording of Mike Oldfield's Hergest Ridge. It's his second album, often overshadowed by its overbearing predecessor Tubular Bells. 

All proceeds from the sale will go to the Disasters and Emergency Committee (DEC) appeal who are providing aid for refugees of the Ukrainian crisis.

You can purchase the album here. Please donate generously!
