Analog

The inspiration for this album was the aquisition of various analog synthesizers. It's often useful to define some constraints in order to stimulate creativity, and so my self-imposed constraint was to use only analog sources.

You can stream the album on all popular streaming platforms

Here is a funky video for Analog 1,  the album's opener, which gives a good taste of what to expect.

For any gear-inclined person out here, the main synths used on this record are the Behringer Model D, the Moog Mother 32, the Moog DFAM, the Arturia Mini Brute, and a few Eurorack modules.
