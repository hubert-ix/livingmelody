Inspirium

Inspirium is an album inspired by the long instrumental albums of the late 70s. It features acoustic and electric guitars, bass guitar, a real church organ, piano, percussions, mandolin, voices, harp, flute, violin, trumpet, and french horn.

You can find it on all the popular streaming platforms, or you can purchase it on Bandcamp 

It is also now available for a (very)  limited run on CD, in a digipack. Use the "buy" button to order your copy.

Here is an extract of part 2, a guitar and flute duet. The flute is played by the very talented Sibylle Marquardt.
